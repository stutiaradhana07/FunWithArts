Terms & Privacy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Studio & Workshop Terms

By booking a workshop at Fun With Art Studio, you agree to our cancellation policy. Full refunds are available up to 48 hours prior to the scheduled session. Cancellations made within 48 hours are eligible for a 50% refund or studio credit.

Pottery involves inherent risks of breakage during the firing process. While we take every precaution, we cannot guarantee that every piece will survive the kiln. In the rare event of a kiln misfire destroying your work, we will offer a complimentary remake session.

2. Online Shop Policies

All items are handcrafted and therefore unique. Slight variations in size, shape, and glaze color are to be expected and celebrated. We do not accept returns based on these natural variations.

If an item arrives damaged, you must contact us with photographic evidence within 48 hours of delivery to qualify for a replacement or refund.

3. Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Fun With Art collects only the information necessary to process your orders and studio bookings (Name, Email, Phone Number, and Shipping Address). We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Your payment information is processed securely through encrypted third-party gateways. We do not store your credit card details on our servers.
