Behind the Clay

Meet the Maker.

Hi, I'm Swetha.

Fun With Art didn't start as a grand business plan. It started as a personal escape—a way to step away from screens, slow down, and get my hands dirty.

There is something incredibly grounding about taking a raw piece of earth and shaping it into something beautiful, functional, and permanent. What began as late-night studio sessions quickly grew into a passion for sharing that exact feeling with others.

Swetha

Slow art for
fast lives.

We live in a world obsessed with instant gratification. Pottery forces you to respect the process. You cannot rush the drying. You cannot rush the kiln. Every single piece in our collection teaches patience.

When you buy a mug, a vase, or book a class with us, you aren't just getting an object. You are getting hours of intention, focus, and a little piece of the slow life to keep in your home.

Ready to get your hands dirty?

Whether you want to shop our latest collection or join me at the wheel, there is a place for you here.
